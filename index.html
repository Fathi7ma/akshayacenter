Akshaya - Gateway of Opportunities

Akshaya has overcome many challenges, attracting those seeking innovation. Launched by Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam on November 18, 2002, Akshaya represents Kerala's commitment to digital progress. It revolutionizes public services and boosts social inclusion through digital transformation.

Mission

  • Bridge the gap between the 'Information Rich and Poor'.
  • Promote e-literacy and provide government services through public-private partnerships.
  • Enhance public service delivery for accessibility in local communities.
  • Transform governance for transparent, efficient services through ICT.

Vision

  • Achieve 100% digital and social inclusion by 'Connecting the Unconnected'.
  • Improve service delivery efficiency at the grassroots level.
  • Foster collaborative relationships with citizens and communities.
  • Utilize government resources to enhance productivity and public communication.

Objectives

  • Leverage ICT to improve services and strengthen government relations with citizens.
